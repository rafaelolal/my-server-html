Guion Bluford

 Guion Bluford, aerospace engineer, Air Force Officer and Pilot, and NASA astronaut. He was the first African-American astronaut to go to space!

Bluford was born in Philadelphia, on November 22nd, 1942. As for college he recieved all the way up to a doctoreate in aerospace engineering. He also served time in the military as an Air Force pilot, with experience in piloting many different vehicles. All this eperience and knowledge allowed him to become a NASA astronaut, being a part of the crew STS-8. As a part of this crew, Bluford became the first astronaut of African-American descent to go to space. In addition, he completed a number of other missions as an astronaut after.
